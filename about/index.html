About

I've been a full-time UX designer for over 15 years in the financial enterprise industry. Part of that time I've belonged to (small) UX teams and at other times I've been a team of one. My focus has been on three areas: user research, design and implementation, and validation. My particular strength is turning requirements into high fidelity prototypes which can be used to communicate the design to stakeholders.

As I've worked closely with development, both organizationally and geographically, I am well versed in AGILE development practice and work alongside software developers in a consulting and often contributory role to drive the look & feel and behaviour of our products to ensure that the final result exceeds the expectations of our consumers.
