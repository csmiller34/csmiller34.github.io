About

I've been a full-time UX designer for over 15 years in the financial enterprise industry. For part of that time I participated in (small) UX teams and at other times I've been a UX team-of-one. Over that time, I focused on user research, design and implementation, and validation. I love transforming customer, business and technical requirements into high fidelity prototypes which can be used to communicate the design to stakeholders.

As I've worked closely with development, both organizationally and geographically, I am well versed in AGILE development practice and work alongside software developers in a consulting and often contributory role to drive the look & feel and behaviour of our products to ensure that the final result exceeds the expectations of our consumers.

This portfolio was developed with the jekyll static site generator. It uses straightforward HTML, CSS, and Javascript with carousel and zoom plugins.
